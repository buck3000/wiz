Your license key will arrive in the Stripe receipt email sent to your inbox. Use it to unlock your Wiz features.

Activate your license

Option 1: Environment variable

Add this to your shell profile (.zshrc, .bashrc, etc.):

export WIZ_LICENSE_KEY="ey...your-key-here"

Option 2: License file

Save your key to a config file:

mkdir -p ~/.config/wiz
echo '{"key":"ey...your-key-here"}' > ~/.config/wiz/license.json

Verify

Check that your tier is active:

wiz status
